Girls Hostel

Safe, comfortable, and empowering

The Girls' Hostel at CUSB is a secure and welcoming residential facility for female students. Offering dining, recreation, wellness support, and round-the-clock security, it ensures that every student feels supported and safe throughout their academic stay.

What's Inside Girls Hostel

🛏️

Residential Rooms

Comfortable, well-furnished rooms with study space, adequate storage, and 24-hour power backup to support academic life.

🍽️

Dining Facility

An in-house dining hall serving hygienic, nutritious, and varied meals — catering to diverse dietary preferences.

🧘

Recreation & Wellness

Dedicated spaces for indoor recreation, yoga, and wellness activities — promoting physical and mental health.

🔐

Safety Infrastructure

Biometric entry, CCTV monitoring, women wardens, and round-the-clock security staff ensure the highest standards of safety.
