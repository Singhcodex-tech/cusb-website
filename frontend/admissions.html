Frequently Asked Questions
Is CUET mandatory for admission to CUSB?
Yes. Admissions to all UG and PG programmes at CUSB are through CUET conducted by NTA. There is no separate entrance exam by the university.
What is the reservation policy?
CUSB follows Government of India reservation policy — SC (15%), ST (7.5%), OBC-NCL (27%), EWS (10%), and horizontal reservations for PwD and other categories.
Is hostel accommodation available?
Yes, separate hostel facilities are available for boys and girls on campus, subject to availability. Allotment is made after admission confirmation.
Where is CUSB located?
Central University of South Bihar is located at SH-7, Panchanpur, Gaya – 824236, Bihar, India.
