About Girls Hostel

Accommodation

Comfortable single and double occupancy rooms with study desks, built-in storage, and 24-hour hot water. Room allotment is handled through the Dean of Students' office.

Dining & Nutrition

In-hostel mess serving nutritionally balanced meals — breakfast, lunch, evening snacks, and dinner. Special dietary accommodations available on request from the warden.

Recreation & Wellness

Indoor recreation room, yoga and meditation corner, and access to a professional counsellor. Regular wellness workshops and cultural activities keep residents engaged.

Safety & Warden System

Exclusively female warden team, biometric entry, high-resolution CCTV, and periodic safety audits. Emergency helpline connected to campus security control room.
