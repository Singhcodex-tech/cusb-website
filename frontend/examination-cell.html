Important Information
Grading System 10-point CGPA system as per UGC guidelines. Minimum passing grade: D (4.0)
Attendance Requirement Minimum 75% attendance in each course is mandatory to appear in end-semester examination
Examination Fee Included in semester fee. Late fee applicable for delayed form submission
Re-evaluation Application within 15 days of result declaration. Fee: ₹500 per paper
Back/Improvement Students can appear in back examination for failed papers in subsequent semesters
Malpractice Any malpractice leads to cancellation of paper / debarment as per university ordinance
